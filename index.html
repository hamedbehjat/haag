BUOYANCY

Haag put its first strong step with "Buoyancy" for creating a different form of rock music. "Buoyancy" was recorded, mixed and mastered in Bell studio in 2014. "Chainsaw", "One Hundred and Seventy-Two" and "Buoyancy" are composed and arranged by Amirhossein Rezaei who is the drummer of the band. Guitar player - Mohsen Rezaei - is composer and arranger of "Eradicated" and "Rear Throw". Aria Pourtabatabaei plays pianos and keyboards of the album. In March 15th 2015, "Buoyancy" released physically on CDs and digitally, with "Avaye Shabahang" label and under license from Ministry of Culture and Islamic Guidance. It made the followers of this genre of music interested in and faced various reactions. Eventually in September 2019, "Buoyancy" released globally in all major online stores.
